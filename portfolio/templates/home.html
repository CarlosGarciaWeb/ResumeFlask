About me

I shifted my career after over 4 years as an Accounts Receivable Specialist to a backend developer. I got into web development by accident when I purchased a web development course on udemy in order to improve at an SEO freelance project I was doing. Then I fell in love with it while researching how to automate a process that required filling out customer web forms at a previous experience in Accounts Receivable. I found out I could do it by using Python and the Selenium Webdriver library, so I bought another course and developed a script that would pull data from excel (with the pandas library) and then fill out the require information through a loop into the web form.

Since then, it became a hobby of mine to study programming after work. In November I took the risk to quit and pursue my dreams. Now I have done several projects in Python with Django and Flask, as well as Golang with Gin, and a couple of projects with Rust. I want to become a backend developer, the best I can, using cloud providers like AWS, GCP, Azure and new serverless technologies like AWS Lambda.
